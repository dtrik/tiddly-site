0. Why Focus Matters

damu 27th July 2024 at 9:31am
Hyperfocus

Everyone is focused on something or the other all the time. Focus is needed not just for productivity but also personal wellbeing according to the author. There are too many distractions now than any point in human history. On average, we get 40 seconds of focus before getting distracted.

Hyperfocus = focusing deeply on one important activity at a time

Our attention is limited, it has to be invested carefully. It improves personal and professional life.

Author recommends the following to better focus on the book itself:

  1. keep phone out of sight
  2. optimize environment towards focus
  3. keep a distraction list to dump things that pop up immediately
  4. question each thing that demands our attention, including the book
  5. coffee or tea
  6. active reading using highlights and notes
  7. when focus wavers, do some other thing and return after recharging
